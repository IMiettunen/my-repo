1. Add money to your wallet
   (or play as a poor person)

2. Choose a number/numbers you want to bet on(separated by comma ",").
   OR choose a color.

3. Choose a bet amount that is suitable for your bankroll
   (Bet will be divided evenly between the numbers you pick)

4. 'Spin the wheel' for numbers, or press the color you chose.

5. Wait for the wheel to spin and reveal the winning number

6. Collect your winnings, if you got lucky

7. Place another bet and try again, or cash out pressing quit


Pay off:
Colors: 2 x bet
Numbers: 35 // (number of chosen numbers) x bet
